Help & Guidance

📋

What documentation do I need?

Most states require a completed exemption certificate (e.g. Form ST-5, ST-119) presented to the vendor before purchase. Keep copies on file for 3–7 years depending on your state.

🔑

How do I get an exemption number?

Register with your state's department of revenue. Most states issue a unique exemption number or certificate to qualifying 501(c)(3) organizations. Some states use your federal EIN.

⚠️

When does exemption NOT apply?

Purchases for personal use, meals at restaurants (in many states), hotel stays for personal travel, and items resold commercially are often taxable even for nonprofits.

🏛️

What if I paid tax by mistake?

File a refund claim with the state revenue agency. Most states allow 3 years to claim a refund. You may need to provide your exemption certificate and purchase receipts.
